## What it is

**Bloom** lets you create and share cross-platform mobile apps in seconds with full backend
deployment. Create functional MVPs, test natively on your phone, and launch real mobile
applications instantly. Hosted at [bloom.diy](https://bloom.diy). Proprietary.

## Best for

- **Users building mobile apps fast** — cross-platform, full backend, test on phone, launch
  instantly.
- **Rapid MVP creation** — from idea to functional mobile app in seconds.

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (browser-only; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Non-mobile apps (it's mobile-focused).
