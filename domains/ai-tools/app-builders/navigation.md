# App Builders — navigation

> You are in: `domains/ai-tools/app-builders/`. This folder covers **AI-powered app/website
> builders** — turnkey SaaS tools that generate a shipped application from a chat description.
> Distinct from `studios/` (human agencies you hire to build custom AI) and `agents/`
> (agent frameworks you run yourself): app-builders are self-serve AI tools that produce a
> deployable app end-to-end.

## What's here

| Path | Type | What it is | Who it's for |
|------|------|------------|--------------|
| [`ideavo.md`](./ideavo.md) | service | **Ideavo** — free AI app builder. Build production-ready websites, mobile apps, games, and internal tools by chatting with AI. BYOK (OpenAI/Anthropic/Google, no markup). Next.js + Expo. Export to GitHub, deploy to Vercel. SOC 2. Freemium ($0/$5/$10). | AI agents (and humans) going from idea to shipped app by chatting — bring your own model API key, export code, deploy. |
| [`stitch.md`](./stitch.md) | service | **Stitch** (by Google) — AI UI generator for mobile and web. Describe what you want, get a UI. Fast design ideation. | Rapid UI ideation; teams wanting a Google-backed AI design tool. AI compat: 2/5. |
| [`openbuilder.md`](./openbuilder.md) | service | **OpenBuilder** — AI app builder. Describe what you'd like to build, get a working app. Public showcase of launched apps (CRMs, tools, stores, etc.). Free. | Rapid app ideation from a description. AI compat: 2/5. |
| [`underwriter.md`](./underwriter.md) | service | **Underwriter** — AI-powered website builder. Describe what you want, get a website. | Quick website generation from a description. AI compat: 2/5. |
