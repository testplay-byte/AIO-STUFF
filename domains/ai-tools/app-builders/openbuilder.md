## What it is

**OpenBuilder** is an AI app builder — describe what you'd like to build and it generates a
working app. Includes a public showcase of what others have launched (landing pages, CRMs,
tools, stores). Hosted at [theopenbuilder.com](https://theopenbuilder.com). Free to use.

## Best for

- **Rapid app ideation from a description** — describe a habit tracker, reading list, budget
  tracker, meal planner, portfolio, travel planner, and get a working app.
- **Exploring what's possible** — the showcase gallery shows real launched examples.

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (browser-only; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Production-scale apps with custom backends (it's for prototyping/ideation).

## Quick facts

- **What it does:** describe an app, get a working build.
- **Showcase:** public gallery of launched apps (Finwise, Reddit Curator, Neumorphism CRM,
  AI Immigration Consultant, Art Gallery, Time Atlas, Furniture Store, etc.).
- **Cost:** free.
- **Platform:** web browser.
- **AI compatibility:** 2/5 — user-facing app builder; an AI agent can recommend it but
  doesn't use it directly.
