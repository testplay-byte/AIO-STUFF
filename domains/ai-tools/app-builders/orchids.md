## What it is

**Orchids** is an AI-powered IDE for building full-stack apps. Chat to build, see your screen,
hear your voice. Download free for Mac, Windows, and Linux. Hosted at
[orchids.app](https://www.orchids.app).

## Best for

- **Users building full-stack apps with AI** — chat to build, visual preview, voice input.
- **Desktop-based AI app development** — native app for Mac/Win/Linux (not browser-only).

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (desktop app; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Browser-based workflows (it's a desktop download).
