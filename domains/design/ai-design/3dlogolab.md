## What it is

**3DLogoLab** converts 2D logos to 3D rotating animations in seconds. Upload your logo, get a
3D animated version. Hosted at [3dlogolab.io](https://www.3dlogolab.io). Proprietary.

## Best for

- **Users wanting a 3D animated logo** — upload a 2D logo, get a 3D rotating animation.
- **Branding + marketing** — 3D logos for videos, presentations, websites.

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (browser-only; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Non-logo 3D work (it's logo-to-3D specifically).
