## What it is

**AppAlchemy Design** is an AI-powered tool for designing beautiful mobile apps. Describe what
you want, get a mobile app design. Hosted at [design.appalchemy.ai](https://design.appalchemy.ai).
Proprietary. Companion to the AppAlchemy app builder.

## Best for

- **Users designing mobile app UIs with AI** — describe the app, get a design starting point.
- **Teams that want AI-assisted mobile design** before building.

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (browser-only; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Non-mobile design (it's mobile-app-focused).
