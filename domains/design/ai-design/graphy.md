## What it is

**Graphy** enables anyone to become a skilled data storyteller by radically simplifying the
way data is presented and communicated. Make beautiful graphs online for free with AI.
Hosted at [graphy.app](https://graphy.app). Freemium.

## Best for

- **Users creating beautiful data visualizations** — AI-assisted graph/chart creation.
- **Data storytelling** — present data in a compelling, visual way.

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (browser-only; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Programmatic charting (use a charting library like Recharts instead).

## Quick facts

- **What it is:** AI-powered data visualization + graph maker.
- **Cost:** freemium.
