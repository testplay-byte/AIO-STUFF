## What it is

**Ilus** is an AI illustration generator — generate consistent, on-brand visuals with
ready-to-use AI styles or custom-trained models. Perfect for designers, marketers, and
creators. Hosted at [ilus.ai](https://ilus.ai). Proprietary.

## Best for

- **Designers/marketers needing consistent AI illustrations** — use ready-made styles or
  train a custom model on your brand for on-brand visuals.
- **Teams wanting illustration consistency** — custom-trained models keep the visual style
  consistent across all generated illustrations.

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (browser-only; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Photo editing (it's illustration generation, not photo manipulation).
