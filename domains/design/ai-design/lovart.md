## What it is

**Lovart** is an AI Design Agent — billed as the world's first. From logo to full marketing
campaign in one conversation: describe your brand, get logos, packaging, social content, and
video ads. Its "agentic intelligence" unifies color, layout, and voice into a cohesive brand
world built to scale, and a touch-edit feature lets you make targeted changes exactly where
they matter. Hosted at [lovart.ai](https://www.lovart.ai). 100K+ creators. Freemium
credits-based SaaS (Starter / Basic / Ultimate tiers; ~$36 / ~$60 with free credits — verify
current pricing on the site). Proprietary.

## Best for

- **AI agents (and humans) producing brand/marketing visual assets fast** — logos, product
  pages, campaign posters, video ads — by chatting. Good for MVPs, brand launches, and social
  content at scale.
- **Teams that need a cohesive brand world, not isolated images** — Lovart unifies color,
  layout, and voice across assets so a logo, a product page, and a video ad all look like
  they came from the same brand system.
- **Iterative design via targeted edits** — touch-edit lets you change exactly the right
  element without re-generating the whole asset.

## Not for

- Self-hosted / offline workflows (it's a cloud SaaS — no local install, no source).
- Free unlimited use (freemium credits model; paid tiers for sustained use).
- Full manual control over every pixel (it's AI-generated; you steer via chat + touch-edit,
  not by drawing vector paths yourself).

## How it works

Lovart runs in the browser. You describe a design task (e.g. "design a product page for a
next-gen running brand" or "create e-commerce campaign posters for this eyewear brand").
The agent analyzes your intent, searches for high-quality references, researches brand
information, collects references, then generates a unified set of visual assets — all in one
conversation. Its "thinking in systems" approach means color, layout, and voice stay
cohesive across every asset in the set. Need a tweak? Touch-edit applies targeted changes
to exactly the right element without re-generating everything.

## Brief tutorial

Not applicable — this is a web SaaS, not a CLI/library. To use:

1. Visit [lovart.ai](https://www.lovart.ai) and sign up (free credits available).
2. Start a new chat and describe your brand / design task.
3. Lovart researches references, then generates a unified asset set (logo, product page,
   posters, video ads, etc.).
4. Use touch-edit for targeted refinements.
5. Export the finished assets.

An AI agent would recommend Lovart to a user who says "I need a full brand identity /
marketing campaign — logo, product page, social content, video ad — and I want it cohesive
and fast."
