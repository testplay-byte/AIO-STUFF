## What it is

**Meshy** is an AI 3D model generator — turns text and images into production-ready 3D models
in 20-30 seconds. Export to FBX, OBJ, GLB, and STL formats. Hosted at
[meshy.ai](https://www.meshy.ai). Freemium.

## Best for

- **Users generating 3D models from text or images** — describe or upload, get a 3D model in
  seconds. Export for games, AR/VR, 3D printing.
- **Rapid 3D prototyping** — skip manual modeling for quick concept models.

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (browser-only; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Precision CAD/engineering models (it's AI-generated, not exact-spec).

## Quick facts
