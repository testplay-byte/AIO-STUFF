## What it is

**Napkin AI** is the visual AI for business storytelling — turns text into visual diagrams,
graphics, and presentations. Hosted at [napkin.ai](https://www.napkin.ai). Proprietary.

## Best for

- **Users creating visual business content** — turn text into diagrams, flowcharts, and visual
  stories with AI.
- **Business storytelling** — make presentations and docs more visual.

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (browser-only; AI compatibility: 2/5).
