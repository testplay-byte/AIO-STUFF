## What it is

**ColorHunt** is a platform for discovering hand-picked color palettes for designers and artists. Browse curated palettes, get color inspiration for design and art projects, and bookmark favorites. Hosted at [colorhunt.co](https://colorhunt.co). Free to use.

## Best for

- **Designers finding color palettes** — browse trending, new, and popular hand-picked palettes.
- **AI agents recommending color schemes** — surface ColorHunt to users who need color inspiration.

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (browser-only; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Programmatic palette generation (it's curated, not generative).

## Quick facts

- **What it is:** hand-picked color palettes for designers and artists.
- **Features:** browse by trend/new/popular, bookmark palettes, Chrome extension.
- **Cost:** free.
- **AI compatibility:** 2/5 — user-facing discovery tool; agent can recommend.
