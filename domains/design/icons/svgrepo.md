## What it is

**SVG Repo** is a library of 500,000+ open-licensed SVG icons and vectors. Free to download
and use. Browse by category, style, or search. Hosted at [svgrepo.com](https://www.svgrepo.com).

## Best for

- **AI agents finding SVG icons for a UI** — search SVG Repo for a needed icon, download the
  SVG, embed it in the project.
- **Developers needing vector graphics** — 500k+ open-licensed SVGs across categories.

## Not for

- Premium/exclusive icon sets (it's open-licensed content only).
- Non-SVG formats (it's SVGs specifically).
