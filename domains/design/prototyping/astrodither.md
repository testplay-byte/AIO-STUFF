## What it is

**AstroDither** is an audio-reactive Three.js / WebGPU experiment by Robert Borghesi exploring dithering and fluid simulation with TSL. A visual art piece — "signal lost, beauty found, digital chaos." Hosted at [astrodither.robertborghesi.is](https://astrodither.robertborghesi.is).
