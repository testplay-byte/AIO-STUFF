## What it is

**AI Particle Simulator** by Casberry India is a high-performance WebGL simulation platform for creative computational artists. Generate, save, and publish massive 3D particle swarms using neural navigation. Hosted at [particles.casberry.in](https://particles.casberry.in).

- **Creative artists generating 3D particle swarm visualizations** — neural navigation drives the particle behavior.
