## What it is

**Penpot** is the open-source design platform for product teams that need scalable
collaboration. The open-source alternative to Figma — design, prototype, and collaborate on
UI/UX in the browser. Self-hostable. ~55k stars, MPL-2.0, Clojure. Hosted at
[penpot.app](https://penpot.app).

## Best for

- **Teams wanting an open-source Figma alternative** — design + prototype + collaborate, with
  the freedom to self-host.
- **Product teams needing scalable collaboration** — real-time multi-user design.

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (browser-based design tool; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Non-design tasks (it's a design/prototyping platform specifically).
