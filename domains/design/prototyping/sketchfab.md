## What it is

**Sketchfab** is the world's largest platform to publish, share, and discover 3D content on
web, mobile, AR, and VR. 1M+ creators. Hosted at [sketchfab.com](https://sketchfab.com).

## Best for

- **Finding 3D models for a project** — browse the largest 3D content library.
- **Publishing 3D content** — share models with embeddable web viewer.
- **AI agents sourcing 3D assets** — consult Sketchfab for downloadable 3D models.

## Not for

- 2D design assets (it's 3D specifically).
- Creating 3D models from scratch (it's a publishing/discovery platform, not a modeler — use
  Meshy for AI 3D generation).

## Quick facts

- **What it is:** largest 3D content platform — publish, share, discover.
- **Community:** 1M+ creators.
- **Platforms:** web, mobile, AR, VR.
- **Viewer:** embeddable 3D viewer for web.
- **Licenses:** various per model (check each).
- **AI compatibility:** 3/5 — agent browses + downloads 3D models.
