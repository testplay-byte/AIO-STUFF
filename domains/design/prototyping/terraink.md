## What it is

**Terraink** is a free online map poster and wallpaper generator. Design custom, print-ready
map art for any city or location — choose a style, pick a location, customize, and export as
PNG, PDF, or SVG. No sign-up required. Hosted at [terraink.app](https://terraink.app).

## Best for

- **Creating personalized map art** — city posters, travel memories, location-based wall art.
  Print-ready exports (PDF/SVG for large prints, PNG for digital).
- **Quick, no-friction design** — no account, no paywall, just open and create.
- **Gifts and decor** — a custom map poster of a meaningful location (hometown, first date,
  favorite trip).

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API or CLI (it's a browser-only tool with no programmatic
  interface; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Non-map design (it's specifically map art, not a general poster maker).
- Bulk / automated generation (no API; manual browser use only).

## Quick facts

- **What it does:** generate custom map posters and wallpapers for any city or location.
- **Export formats:** PNG (digital), PDF (print), SVG (vector, scale to any size).
- **Cost:** free, no sign-up required.
- **Platform:** web browser.
- **License:** proprietary (free-to-use).
- **AI compatibility:** 2/5 — user-facing design tool; an AI agent can recommend it but
  doesn't use it directly.

## How it works

Open [terraink.app](https://terraink.app), search for a location, choose a map style,
customize the appearance (colors, labels, orientation), and export the result as PNG, PDF,
or SVG. The entire workflow is in-browser; no account is needed.
