## What it is

**UpsideOnly** is a paper-trading platform where you trade real markets (global equity,
commodity, forex, and crypto) with virtual money — no real money at risk. Their AI (trained
on 22B+ trades) ranks your trading edge, and you earn real cash two ways: get backed with
their capital (you keep a share of the upside) or win weekly leaderboard prizes. Hosted at
[upsideonly.com](https://upsideonly.com). Free to use, 110K+ traders, by A Perpetuals.com
Group Company (NASDAQ:PDC). Proprietary SaaS.

## Best for

- **Users who want to prove their trading strategy risk-free** — paper-trade with a $100k
  virtual balance across real markets; if the AI ranks your edge highly, you get backed with

- **Traders who want to earn cash without risking their own money** — weekly leaderboard

## How it works

You sign up free and get a $100,000 virtual balance. You paper-trade any market (equity,
commodity, forex, crypto) using real market data and charts. The platform's AI — trained on
22B+ trades — analyzes your trading patterns, filters the noise, and ranks your edge on a
10-point scale plus a 7-day percentile rank. If you rank highly, two things happen: (1) you
can get "backed" — the platform uses its own capital to execute your strategy in real
markets, and you keep 50% of the profits; (2) you compete on a weekly leaderboard where the
best paper-trading performance wins cash payouts. You never risk your own money trading.
