## What it is

**StudyFetch** is an AI-powered study platform that turns course materials (lectures, PDFs,
notes) into interactive study sets — flashcards, quizzes, and notes. Hosted at
[studyfetch.com](https://www.studyfetch.com). Freemium.

## Best for

- **Students studying from course materials** — upload lectures/PDFs/notes, get AI-generated
  flashcards + quizzes + study notes.
- **AI-assisted learning** — turns passive materials into active study tools.

## Not for

- AI agents looking for an API (browser-only; AI compatibility: 2/5).
- Programming-specific learning (use Exercism in dev-tools/learning/ for that).
